# Telegram Writing Rules

## Purpose

This document defines how selected products should be presented in Hebrew Telegram posts.

The goal is to produce clear, useful, trustworthy, and engaging posts that feel like recommendations from a knowledgeable deals editor—not generic AI copy and not aggressive advertising.

This document controls writing style only. It does not decide whether a product should be selected.

---

## Audience

The primary audience is Hebrew-speaking consumers in Israel who are interested in:

- Useful products.
- Good deals.
- Smart gadgets.
- Home, kitchen, car, travel, kids, pet, and electronics products.
- Products that are easy to understand and purchase quickly.

The writing should be accessible to a broad audience and should avoid unnecessary technical jargon.

---

## Core Writing Principle

Every post should quickly answer three questions:

1. What is the product?
2. Why should the reader care?
3. What should the reader do next?

The product benefit should appear before long specifications.

---

## Tone

The tone should be:

- Natural.
- Friendly.
- Clear.
- Useful.
- Confident without exaggeration.
- Enthusiastic when justified.
- Written in modern, fluent Hebrew.
- Suitable for a Telegram deals channel.

Avoid:

- Robotic language.
- Literal translation from English.
- Corporate marketing language.
- Excessive hype.
- Fake urgency.
- Repetitive opening phrases.
- Overuse of emojis.
- Claims that cannot be verified from product data.

---

## Recommended Post Structure

A standard post should generally follow this order:

### 1. Hook

Open with a short line that communicates the main benefit, use case, or problem.

Good approaches:

- A common frustration.
- A useful result.
- A surprising function.
- A clear deal angle.
- A gift or seasonal use case.

Examples:

- נמאס לכם מכבלים שמסתובבים על השולחן?
- פתרון קטן שיכול לעשות הרבה סדר במטבח.
- מטען אחד במקום כמה מטענים בתיק.
- שימושי במיוחד למי שנוסע הרבה ברכב.
- מוצר פשוט, אבל כזה שבאמת חוסך זמן.

Do not start with a long technical product name.

### 2. Product Explanation

Explain in one or two short sentences:

- What the product does.
- Where it is useful.
- What makes it different or valuable.

### 3. Key Benefits

Include only the most relevant benefits.

Prefer 2–4 concise benefits, such as:

- Easy to use.
- Compact.
- Rechargeable.
- Suitable for travel.
- Multiple ports.
- Waterproof.
- Space saving.
- Strong battery.
- Useful for home, car, office, or kids.

Do not copy the full AliExpress specification list.

### 4. Trust or Deal Signal

When supported by data, mention one or two of:

- Rating.
- Number of orders.
- Recognizable brand.
- Discount.
- Current price.
- Free shipping.
- Coupon.
- Strong review history.

Do not invent a rating, order count, previous price, discount, or shipping condition.

### 5. Price

Present the price clearly.

Preferred formats:

- מחיר: כ־59 ₪
- עכשיו ב־$18.90
- מחיר מבצע: כ־89 ₪

When displaying a converted price, make it clear that the value is approximate.

Do not claim that it is the cheapest price available unless that has been independently verified.

### 6. Call to Action

End with a simple action.

Examples:

- לצפייה במוצר 👇
- לפרטים ולרכישה 👇
- לבדיקה באליאקספרס 👇
- שווה להציץ לפני שהמחיר משתנה 👇

Avoid fake scarcity such as:

- נשארו רק 2!
- נגמר בעוד דקה!
- כולם רצים לקנות!

unless the system has reliable real-time evidence.

---

## Length

The default post should be concise.

Recommended target:

- 250–650 Hebrew characters before the link.
- Usually 4–8 short lines.
- No large text blocks.
- No unnecessary repetition.

A complex, expensive, or technical product may receive a slightly longer explanation, but clarity remains more important than detail.

---

## Formatting

Use short paragraphs and line breaks.

A recommended format:

```text
פתרון קטן שעושה הרבה סדר בכבלים 🔌

מארגן כבלים שנצמד לשולחן ושומר על כבלי הטעינה במקום, בלי שהם ייפלו לרצפה או יסתבכו.

✓ מתאים למספר כבלים
✓ התקנה פשוטה
✓ שימושי לבית ולמשרד

מחיר: כ־18 ₪

לצפייה במוצר 👇
```

Use bullet symbols consistently.

Recommended symbols:

- ✓
- •
- 🔹

Do not mix many bullet styles in the same post.

---

## Emoji Rules

Emojis should support readability, not replace language.

Recommended:

- 1 emoji in the hook.
- 0–1 emoji near the price.
- 1 emoji near the call to action.
- Usually no more than 3–5 emojis per post.

Avoid:

- Long emoji chains.
- Emoji on every line.
- Repeated fire, alarm, or explosion emojis.
- Emojis unrelated to the product.

---

## Language Rules

- Write in fluent Hebrew.
- Prefer common Israeli wording.
- Keep recognized brand names in English.
- Keep technical terms in English only when the Hebrew alternative is unclear or unnatural.
- Explain unfamiliar technical terms briefly.
- Avoid awkward transliteration.
- Avoid English sentence structure translated directly into Hebrew.
- Avoid gendered wording when a neutral alternative is easy.
- Use punctuation naturally.

---

## Product Names

Do not use the full AliExpress title as the post headline.

Convert long titles into a short human-readable product name.

Example:

Original:

`UGREEN 200W 25000mAh Portable Power Bank PD Fast Charging...`

Preferred:

`סוללת גיבוי UGREEN בהספק 200W`

Preserve important model or brand information only when useful.

---

## Claims and Accuracy

Only state claims supported by the input data.

Never invent:

- Product capabilities.
- Compatibility.
- Materials.
- Warranty.
- Shipping speed.
- Stock level.
- Coupon availability.
- Discount percentage.
- Previous price.
- Local comparison price.
- Rating.
- Number of sales.
- User reviews.
- Safety certifications.

When data is uncertain, omit the claim.

---

## Price and Currency

- Use the product's actual currency when available.
- A shekel conversion may be presented as approximate.
- Do not perform hidden or unsupported conversions.
- Do not present an approximate price as exact.
- Do not confuse sale price with original price.

Preferred wording:

- כ־59 ₪
- בערך 79 ₪
- $18.90 נכון לזמן הפרסום

---

## Brand Handling

When a known brand exists, mention it naturally.

Examples:

- מטען של UGREEN
- סוללת גיבוי Baseus
- אוזניות SoundPEATS

Do not suggest that a product is official, original, or authorized unless the listing data supports that claim.

Avoid emphasizing unknown brands unless the brand name helps identify the product.

---

## Category-Specific Guidance

### Home and Kitchen

Focus on:

- Convenience.
- Organization.
- Cleaning.
- Space saving.
- Everyday use.

### Electronics and Charging

Focus on:

- Number of ports.
- Charging speed.
- Battery capacity.
- Device compatibility.
- Portability.

Avoid technical overload.

### Car Accessories

Focus on:

- Safety.
- Organization.
- Cleaning.
- Emergency use.
- Ease of installation.

Do not make safety claims that are not supported.

### Kids and Baby

Focus on:

- Practical use.
- Parental convenience.
- Clear age suitability when known.

Do not make medical or safety guarantees.

### Pets

Focus on:

- Cleaning.
- Feeding.
- Travel.
- Organization.
- Comfort.

### Outdoor and Travel

Focus on:

- Portability.
- Durability.
- Compact size.
- Battery life.
- Multi-use value.

---

## Diversity in Writing

Avoid producing posts that all sound the same.

Vary the opening style between:

- Problem-first.
- Benefit-first.
- Deal-first.
- Use-case-first.
- Surprise-first.
- Gift-first.

Do not repeatedly open every post with:

- מצאנו לכם...
- מוצר מטורף...
- חובה בכל בית...
- דיל שלא רואים כל יום...

---

## Prohibited Writing Patterns

Do not use:

- Fake urgency.
- Fake scarcity.
- Unsupported superlatives.
- Aggressive pressure.
- Misleading local price comparisons.
- Fabricated personal experience.
- Fabricated reviews.
- Claims such as "הכי טוב", "הכי זול", or "מושלם" without evidence.
- Excessive exclamation marks.
- Large blocks of specifications.
- Repeated calls to action.
- Hashtag spam.

---

## Suggested Output Template

```text
{HOOK}

{SHORT_PRODUCT_EXPLANATION}

{BENEFIT_1}
{BENEFIT_2}
{OPTIONAL_BENEFIT_3}

{OPTIONAL_TRUST_SIGNAL}
מחיר: {PRICE}

לצפייה במוצר 👇
```

The affiliate link and Telegram button should be added by application code, not generated as part of the copy.

---

## Quality Check Before Publishing

Before accepting a generated post, verify:

- The product is correctly identified.
- The hook reflects the real benefit.
- The text does not invent information.
- The price is correct.
- The post is concise.
- The Hebrew sounds natural.
- The post does not repeat itself.
- The post is not excessively promotional.
- The call to action is clear.
- The text does not contain an affiliate URL.
